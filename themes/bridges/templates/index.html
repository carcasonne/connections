welcome to my corner of the wired. this is where i archive the fragments that matter - books that shaped my thinking, articles worth preserving, links to signal among the noise.

the internet feels increasingly ephemeral. platforms disappear, links rot, algorithms decide what survives. this site is my small act of resistance against that entropy. everything here persists as long as i choose to maintain it.

personal reading log. tracking what i've consumed, what i'm working through, what got abandoned. no social aspects, no algorithmic recommendations - just honest accounting of my literary wanderings.

longer-form thoughts on digital ownership, personal archiving, and reclaiming agency in an increasingly platform-mediated world.

on digital collecting

this site exists because i believe in digital ownership. not the artificial scarcity of NFTs or the gamification of streaming platforms, but actual possession of files, control over access, independence from corporate whims.

everything here is self-hosted, self-maintained, deliberately small-scale. it's not optimized for growth or engagement metrics. it's optimized for persistence and personal utility.

take what you like, discard the rest, move on. same philosophy applies to the books, the links, the ideas. nothing here claims to be definitive or complete.
